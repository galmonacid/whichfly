# Product Vision – whichFly

## Vision

Help fly fishers make the **right fly choice in the moment**, using local context, conditions and experience — not guesswork.

whichFly is a **streamside decision-support tool**.

---

## Core principles

1. **Here and now first**
2. **One clear recommendation beats many options**
3. **Context over precision**
4. **Explain the why**
5. **User stays in control**

---

## Primary usage mode: Right now

- User is physically at the river
- Date and time are implicit (now)
- Location is inferred via GPS
- River is suggested, not assumed

---

## Secondary usage mode: Planning

- Explicit date
- Explicit river
- Used before the trip
- Not the primary design target

---

## Target user

- UK-based fly fisher
- River trout
- Intermediate or improving
- Wants confidence and learning, not noise

---

## Non-goals

- Predicting exact hatches
- Replacing local guides
- Optimising for desktop or long sessions
