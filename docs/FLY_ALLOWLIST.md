# Fly Allowlist (MVP) — whichFly

This allowlist constrains the LLM to recommend only **generic, widely-known fly patterns**.
No brand names. No obscure local patterns. This is a trust and safety guardrail.

## How it is used
- The LLM MUST choose `primary.pattern` and `alternatives[].pattern` from this list only.
- If conditions are uncertain, prefer conservative patterns (especially nymphs).
- Sizes must remain in reasonable ranges for UK trout.

---

## Dry flies (surface)

- Adams
- F-Fly
- Klinkhamer
- Elk Hair Caddis
- CDC Sedge

**Typical sizes:** 12–18

---

## Emergers / Soft-hackles

- Klinkhamer (also acceptable as emerger)
- CDC Emerger (generic)
- Partridge and Orange

**Typical sizes:** 12–18

---

## Nymphs (subsurface)

- Pheasant Tail Nymph
- Hare’s Ear Nymph
- Zebra Midge
- Frenchie
- Copper John

**Typical sizes:** 12–20

---

## Streamers (when higher water / low visibility)

- Woolly Bugger
- Generic Baitfish Streamer

**Typical sizes:** 6–12

---

## Notes
- The product does NOT claim specific hatches in MVP.
- The agent may say “general spring conditions often favour…” but must not assert “a BWO hatch is happening” without evidence.
